Who are we?

We are a couple of students fed up with the time spending countless hours finding internships that we want. 

With our weekly email updates, you can get handpicked job postings on your phone, tablet or laptop. 

Our job postings are sourced from company sites, private and public job boards and from Facebook groups. We source and curate our jobs by hand and with the help of automated tools. Every postings is checked to make sure that it is an internship that we would want to apply for ourselves.

Frequently Asked Questions

When will my city/university be supported?

We have a variety of companies and startups in our weekly postings, all of whom are hiring software engineers. We usually steer clear of companies that are smaller than 10 employees unless we are able to vet them otherwise. To give you an idea, just a few of the companies in one of our recent batches included Microsoft, Amazon, Axiom Zen, A Thinking Ape, SAP, and other local and international tech companies. Currently our postings are only for software internships but we hope to change that as we grow.
